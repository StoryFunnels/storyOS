## Problem
A standalone space document (e.g. "Document Name" in the sidebar) has no management
actions — you can't rename it, delete it, or move it to another space/folder. The title
also reads a placeholder "Document Name" with no obvious way to edit it.

## What
- Sidebar context menu (right-click / ⋯) on a document: **Rename**, **Delete**
  (with confirm), **Move to space/folder**, maybe **Duplicate**.
- Editable title inline on the document page (click the title to rename); persist.
- Delete removes it from the sidebar and routes away safely.

## Acceptance
- [ ] Rename a document from the sidebar and from its page title.
- [ ] Delete a document (confirm) — it disappears and navigation doesn't break.
- [ ] Move a document between spaces/folders.
